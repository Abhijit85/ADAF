**Calculation**:
1. **Identify the Total Debt for Each Year**:
   - Total Debt in 2015: $28.5 billion
   - Total Debt in 2014: $29.5 billion

2. **Calculate the Change in Total Debt**:
   \[
   \text{Change in Debt} = \text{Total Debt in 2015} - \text{Total Debt in 2014} = \$28.5 \text{ billion} - \$29.5 \text{ billion} = -\$1.0 \text{ billion}
   \]

3. **Calculate the Percentage Change**:
   \[
   \text{Percentage Change} = \left( \frac{\text{Change in Debt}}{\text{Total Debt in 2014}} \right) \times 100 = \left( \frac{-\$1.0 \text{ billion}}{\$29.5 \text{ billion}} \right) \times 100
   \]

   \[
   \text{Percentage Change} = \left( \frac{-1.0}{29.5} \right) \times 100 \approx -3.39\%
   \]
