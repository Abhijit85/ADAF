
=== FINAL SUMMARY ===

**Calculation**:
1. **Identify Base**: The base year is 2013, with a free cash flow of $2085 million.
2. **Identify Part**: The free cash flow for 2014 is $1504 million.
3. **Calculate Change in Free Cash Flow**:
   \[
   \text{Change} = \text{Free Cash Flow in 2014} - \text{Free Cash Flow in 2013} = 1504 - 2085 = -581 \text{ million}
   \]
4. **Calculate Percentage Change**:
   \[
   \text{Percentage Change} = \left(\frac{\text{Change}}{\text{Free Cash Flow in 2013}}\right) \times 100 = \left(\frac{-581}{2085}\right) \times 100
   \]
5. **Perform the Division**:
   \[
   \frac{-581}{2085} \approx -0.2786
   \]
6. **Convert to Percentage**:
   \[

   \]
