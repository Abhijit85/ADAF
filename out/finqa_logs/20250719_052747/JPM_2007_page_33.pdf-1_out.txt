   \[
   \text{Change} = \text{2006 fees} - \text{2005 fees} = 5520 - 4088 = 1432 \text{ million}
   \]

   \[
   \text{Percentage Change} = \left(\frac{\text{Change}}{\text{2005 fees}}\right) \times 100 = \left(\frac{1432}{4088}\right) \times 100
