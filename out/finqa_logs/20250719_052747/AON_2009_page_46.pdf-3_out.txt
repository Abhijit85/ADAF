
=== FINAL SUMMARY ===

**Question**: What was the percent of the change in the risk and insurance brokerage services segment revenue from 2008 to 2009?

**Calculation**:
1. **Identify the Revenue for Each Year**:
   - Revenue for 2009: $6305 million
   - Revenue for 2008: $6197 million

2. **Calculate the Change in Revenue**:
   \[
   \text{Change in Revenue} = \text{Revenue in 2009} - \text{Revenue in 2008} = 6305 - 6197 = 108 \text{ million}
   \]

3. **Calculate the Percentage Change**:
   \[
   \text{Percentage Change} = \left(\frac{\text{Change in Revenue}}{\text{Revenue in 2008}}\right) \times 100 = \left(\frac{108}{6197}\right) \times 100
   \]

4. **Perform the Division and Multiplication**:
   \[
   \frac{108}{6197} \approx 0.01743
   \]
   \[
   0.01743 \times 100 \approx 1.743\%
   \]

**Answer**: The percent change in the risk and insurance brokerage services segment revenue from 2008 to 2009 is approximately 1.74%.
