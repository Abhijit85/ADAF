**Calculation**:
1. **Identify the Revenues**:
   - 2002 net revenue: $922.9 million
   - 2003 net revenue: $973.7 million

2. **Calculate the Difference in Revenue**:
   \[
   \text{Difference} = \$973.7 \, \text{million} - \$922.9 \, \text{million} = \$50.8 \, \text{million}
   \]

3. **Calculate the Growth Rate**:
   \[
   \text{Growth Rate} = \left( \frac{\$50.8 \, \text{million}}{\$922.9 \, \text{million}} \right) \times 100
   \]

4. **Perform the Division**:
   \[
   \frac{\$50.8}{\$922.9} \approx 0.055
   \]

5. **Convert to Percentage**:
   \[
   0.055 \times 100 = 5.5\%
   \]

**Answer**: The growth rate in net revenue for Entergy Louisiana, Inc. in 2003 is 5.5%.
