**Calculation**:

   - **Tier 1 Capital (2007)**: $82.0 billion
   - **Total Capital (Tier 1 and Tier 2) (2007)**: $121.6 billion

2. **Calculate Tier 2 Capital for Each Year**:
   - **Tier 2 Capital (2008)**:
     \[
     \text{Tier 2 Capital (2008)} = \text{Total Capital (2008)} - \text{Tier 1 Capital (2008)}
     \]
     \[
     \text{Tier 2 Capital (2008)} = 108.4 - 71.0 = 37.4 \text{ billion dollars}
     \]

   - **Tier 2 Capital (2007)**:
     \[
     \text{Tier 2 Capital (2007)} = \text{Total Capital (2007)} - \text{Tier 1 Capital (2007)}
     \]
     \[
     \text{Tier 2 Capital (2007)} = 121.6 - 82.0 = 39.6 \text{ billion dollars}
     \]

3. **Calculate the Ratio of Tier 2 Capital (2008) to Tier 2 Capital (2007)**:
   \[
   \text{Ratio} = \frac{\text{Tier 2 Capital (2008)}}{\text{Tier 2 Capital (2007)}}
   \]
   \[
   \text{Ratio} = \frac{37.4}{39.6} \approx 0.944
   \]

**Answer**: 0.944
