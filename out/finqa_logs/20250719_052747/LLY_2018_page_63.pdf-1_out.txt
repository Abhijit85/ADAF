
=== FINAL SUMMARY ===

**Question**: What was the percentage change in inventories between 2017 and 2018?

**Calculation**:
1. **Identify Base and Part**:
   - Inventories in 2017: $4458.3 million
   - Inventories in 2018: $4111.8 million

2. **Calculate the Change**:
   - Change in inventories = Inventories in 2018 - Inventories in 2017
   - Change = $4111.8 million - $4458.3 million = -$346.5 million

3. **Calculate the Percentage Change**:
   - Percentage Change = (Change / Inventories in 2017) × 100
   - Percentage Change = (-$346.5 million / $4458.3 million) × 100
   - Percentage Change = -7.77%

**Answer**: The percentage change in inventories between 2017 and 2018 was -7.77%, indicating a 7.77% decrease.
