**Question**: What was the percentage change in raw materials and supplies between 2017 and 2018?

**Calculation**:
1. **Identify Base (2017 Value)**: $488.8 million
2. **Identify Part (2018 Value)**: $506.5 million
3. **Calculate the Change**: 
   - Change = 2018 Value - 2017 Value
   - Change = $506.5 million - $488.8 million = $17.7 million
4. **Calculate the Percentage Change**:
   - Percentage Change = (Change / 2017 Value) × 100
   - Percentage Change = ($17.7 million / $488.8 million) × 100
   - Percentage Change = 0.0362 × 100
   - Percentage Change = 3.62%

**Answer**: 3.62%
