
=== FINAL SUMMARY ===

**Question**: At December 31, what was the percentage change in investment income from 2011 to 2012?

**Calculation**:
1. **Identify Base**: The investment income for 2011 was $620.0 million.
2. **Identify Part**: The investment income for 2012 was $600.2 million.
3. **Calculate the Change**: 
   \[
   \text{Change} = \text{Investment Income in 2012} - \text{Investment Income in 2011} = 600.2 - 620.0 = -19.8 \text{ million}
   \]
4. **Calculate the Percentage Change**:
   \[
   \text{Percentage Change} = \left(\frac{\text{Change}}{\text{Investment Income in 2011}}\right) \times 100 = \left(\frac{-19.8}{620.0}\right) \times 100
   \]
   \[
   \text{Percentage Change} = -3.19\%
   \]

**Answer**: The investment income decreased by 3.19% from 2011 to 2012.
