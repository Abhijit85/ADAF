**Calculation**:

3. **Calculate the Change**:
   \[
   \text{Change} = \text{Value in 2015} - \text{Value in 2014} = 17430.8 - 16831.9 = 598.9 \text{ million}
   \]

   \[
   \text{Percentage Change} = \left(\frac{\text{Change}}{\text{Value in 2014}}\right) \times 100 = \left(\frac{598.9}{16831.9}\right) \times 100
   \]
5. **Perform the Division**:
   \[
   \frac{598.9}{16831.9} \approx 0.0356
   \]
