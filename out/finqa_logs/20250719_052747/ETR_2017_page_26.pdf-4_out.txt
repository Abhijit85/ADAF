**Calculation**:
1. **Identify the Net Revenues**:

2. **Calculate the Change in Net Revenue**:
   \[
   \text{Change in Net Revenue} = \text{2016 Net Revenue} - \text{2015 Net Revenue} = 1542 - 1666 = -124 \text{ million}
   \]

3. **Calculate the Growth Rate**:
   \[
   \text{Growth Rate} = \left(\frac{\text{Change in Net Revenue}}{\text{2015 Net Revenue}}\right) \times 100 = \left(\frac{-124}{1666}\right) \times 100
   \]

4. **Perform the Division and Multiplication**:
   \[
   \frac{-124}{1666} \approx -0.0744
   \]
   \[
   -0.0744 \times 100 = -7.44\%
   \]
