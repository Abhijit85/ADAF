**Question**: What is the percent change in fair value between 2010 and 2011?

**Calculation**:
1. **Identify Base**: The fair value in 2010 is $81881 million.
2. **Identify Part**: The fair value in 2011 is $99832 million.
3. **Calculate the Change**: 
   \[
   \text{Change} = \text{Fair value in 2011} - \text{Fair value in 2010} = 99832 - 81881 = 17951
   \]
4. **Calculate the Percent Change**:
   \[
   \text{Percent Change} = \left(\frac{\text{Change}}{\text{Fair value in 2010}}\right) \times 100 = \left(\frac{17951}{81881}\right) \times 100
   \]
5. **Perform the Division**:
   \[
   \frac{17951}{81881} \approx 0.2192
   \]
6. **Convert to Percentage**:
   \[
   0.2192 \times 100 = 21.92\%
   \]

**Answer**: The percent change in fair value between 2010 and 2011 is 21.92%.
