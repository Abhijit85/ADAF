**Question**: What is the percent change of securities between 2008 and 2009?

**Calculation**:
1. **Identify the Fair Values**:
   - Fair value in 2009: $72699 million
   - Fair value in 2008: $54163 million

2. **Calculate the Change in Fair Value**:
   \[
   \text{Change} = \text{Fair value in 2009} - \text{Fair value in 2008} = 72699 - 54163 = 18536 \text{ million}
   \]

3. **Calculate the Percent Change**:
   \[
   \text{Percent Change} = \left(\frac{\text{Change}}{\text{Fair value in 2008}}\right) \times 100 = \left(\frac{18536}{54163}\right) \times 100
   \]

4. **Perform the Division and Multiplication**:
   \[
   \frac{18536}{54163} \approx 0.3422
   \]
   \[
   0.3422 \times 100 \approx 34.22\%
   \]
