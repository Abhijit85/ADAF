**Question**: Is money from Jersey legal tender in the UK?

**Factual Answer**: Money from Jersey, specifically the Jersey pound, is not considered legal tender in the United Kingdom. Although Jersey issues its own currency, which is pegged at par with the British pound, it is not recognized as legal tender in the UK.
