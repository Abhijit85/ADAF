**Factual Answer**: Tomás González had notable performances at both the Central American and Caribbean Championships and the 1983 Ibero-American Championships. At the 1983 Ibero-American Championships in Barcelona, Spain, González excelled by winning 1st place in the 200 m event with a time of 20.91 and in the 4 × 400 m relay with a time of 3:07.05.
