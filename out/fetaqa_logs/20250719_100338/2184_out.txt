**Question**: What roles has Gurnam Singh served?

**Factual Answer**: Gurnam Singh served as the Chief Minister of Punjab during two separate terms. His first term was from 8 March 1967 to 25 November 1967, during which he represented the Akali Dal - Sant Fateh Singh party in coalition with the Bharatiya Jana Sangh (BJS) and the Communist Party of India (CPI). His second term was from 17 February 1969 to 27 March 1970, representing the Shiromani Akali Dal in coalition with the BJS.
