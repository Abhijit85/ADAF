**Factual Answer**: Between 1857 and 1858, significant changes occurred in the U.S. Senate involving Thomas J. Rusk, J. Pinckney Henderson, and Matthias Ward. Thomas J. Rusk, a Democratic Senator, died on July 29, 1857. Following his death, J. Pinckney Henderson was appointed as his successor on November 9, 1857.
